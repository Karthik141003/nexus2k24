About NEXUS Hackathon

Ever wonder what happens when creativity collides with curiosity? Get ready for a program that's not just an event - it's an arena where creative minds collaborate to engineer solutions that seamlessly integrate electronics, data transmission, and communication technologies, envisioning a future where interconnected devices enhance our daily lives. Yes! Embark on a captivating journey through the realms of knowledge through the national level hackathon program NEXUS. So what next? Dive into cutting-edge projects that blend technology with real-world connectivity. Our motive is to encourage creativity, innovation, and rapid development, making this hackathon a dynamic platform for all the aspirants to showcase their skills and build something new. Come on guys! Grab your chances tight and let us celebrate the remarkable spirit of competition that has fueled our collective pursuit of excellence.

Registration Guidelines

  1. Form a team of four members.
  2. Submit your project idea and present a ppt for evaluation in the semifinalist selection process.
  3. The prototype of your project will undergo evaluation for the finalist selection.
  4. Certificates will be awarded to both semifinalists and finalists of the event.
  5. The top three finalists will receive cash prizes as recognition for their outstanding contributions.
